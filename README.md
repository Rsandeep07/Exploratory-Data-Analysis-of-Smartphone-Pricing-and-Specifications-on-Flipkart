---
## Overview
---

This project focuses on analyzing smartphone pricing and specifications on Flipkart using data collected through web scraping.
The goal is to study market trends, understand feature–price relationships, and extract insights across various smartphone brands and models available on the platform.

---
## Objectives
--- 
Collect smartphone data from Flipkart using web scraping.
Perform data cleaning and preprocessing to extract structured information.
Analyze key attributes such as price, RAM, storage, battery, ratings, and processor.
Visualize pricing trends, brand comparisons, and specification patterns through EDA.
Identify factors influencing smartphone pricing in the Indian market.

---
## Dataset Information
---
The dataset was created by scraping real-time smartphone listings from Flipkart.

---
## Key Attributes
---
Brand
Model Name
Price (₹)
Ratings
RAM
Storage
Processor
Battery Capacity
Camera Details

The raw scraped data was cleaned, formatted, and transformed to make it ready for analysis.

---
## Insights & Findings
---
Budget and mid-range smartphones dominate Flipkart’s marketplace.
Higher RAM & Storage consistently show a strong influence on price.
Several brands offer high specifications at lower price points, improving value-for-money.
Ratings do not always correlate with higher pricing — some budget devices receive strong user ratings.
Price variation across brands highlights differences in product strategy and target segments.

## Dashboard / Analysis Features
--- 
This EDA includes:
Distribution Analysis — price ranges, ratings, RAM, storage.
Brand Comparison — product counts and average pricing by brand.
